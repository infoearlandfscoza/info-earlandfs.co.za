About Us

At Earland Financial Services, we specialize in simplifying the complex world of vehicle tracking and insurance brokerage. With a team of passionate professionals and years of hands-on experience, our goal is simple: to save you time, money, and stress — while delivering unmatched peace of mind. We proudly partner with over 10 leading vehicle tracking providers and more than 20 trusted insurance companies across South Africa. This allows us to compare multiple options, ensuring you always receive the best deal tailored to your needs — at no cost to you. Whether you're looking for the most affordable car tracking solution, trying to upgrade an outdated tracking unit, or seeking a better insurance rate, our agents work for you. We’ll handle everything — from quote comparisons to appointment bookings, cancellations, and renewals — so you never have to worry about paperwork or phone calls again.
